What problem does AI solve?

AI can improve on all kinds of aspects of sport, for example: coaching, training and streaming. It can help with coaching by giving extremely detailed results and solutions. It can help with training by telling the player exactly what to do based on their performance. And lastly it can inprove streaming of sport events by deciding at what best time to broadcast the video.
